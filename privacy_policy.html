Privacy Policy

Most of Reacji Read Receipts stays in slack. The only data we use of yours is for the purposes of using Reacji Read Receipts within slack, and anonymized usage data from the app for feedback to improve it.

Stored Data

We store credentials from slack for use in adding images to your messages when invoked, finding messages you have reacted to from the channel history, and finding messages in the reaction history. We do not access or store your email address at any time.

Explicitly NOT stored data

Reacji Read Receipts has very little access to your team's data: Reacji Read Receipts only accesses messages from the Slack API that have been reacted to, and these are not stored anywhere on our servers or elsewhere.

Analytics

Simple Poll uses analytics tools such as Dashbot, Google Analytics to monitor performance and to improve the user experience. Collected statistics are, for example, in the form of frequency of use of the key emoji over time.

Third Party Services

Simple Poll is run on Gomix. Please refer to the Heroku Privacy Policy for more details: https://gomix.com/legal/#privacy

Advertising

Reacji Read Receipts does not display any advertisements, and does not share any data with advertisers.

Policy

This privacy policy will change over time.
